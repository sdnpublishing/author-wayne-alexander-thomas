About Wayne Alexander Thomas

WA

Wayne Alexander Thomas

Wayne Alexander Thomas writes about what drives people — the intersecting currents of motivation, leadership, and the deep human need for meaningful work and purposeful action.

His books are grounded in both research and practice, offering frameworks that are intellectually sound and immediately applicable.

For leaders, coaches, and anyone trying to understand and harness the sources of human drive.
